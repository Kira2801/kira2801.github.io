Stella Gale: The Trials Of Faith

System Design · Gameplay Design

Role Game Designer
Focus System Design, Gameplay Design
Year 2026
Engine Unity
01 — Overview

What is Stella Gale?

Stella Gale: The Trials Of Faith is an action-adventure game built in Unity. Players navigate arena-based combat encounters, facing enemies with distinct behavioral patterns across a series of escalating trials. The game is available on Steam.

View on Steam
02 — Design Challenge

The Problem

Designing scalable arena combat systems that feel fair, readable, and increasingly challenging — without becoming repetitive or overwhelming. Enemy behaviors needed to be modular enough to combine interestingly while remaining predictable enough for player counterplay.

  • How do we design enemy behaviors that are readable but not trivial?
  • How do we scale arena difficulty across a full trial arc?
  • How do we document systems clearly enough for a cross-discipline team?
03 — Design Solution

My Approach

I designed a modular enemy behavior system with clearly defined aggression states, attack telegraphs, and cooldown windows. Arena layouts were built to support 1v1, 1vMany, and mixed encounter types with dedicated spawn and navigation zones.

"Every enemy archetype was defined by a single dominant behavior — making them immediately readable while creating interesting combinations when paired with others."

04 — Design Process

How I Got There

  • Created and implemented Level Arena layouts directly in Unity
  • Wrote detailed design documents for Minigames and Side Quests
  • Documented Enemy Behavior specs for the programming team
  • Designed encounter escalation curves across all trial stages
  • Collaborated closely with artists on arena visual language and readability
05 — Result

Outcome

The game shipped successfully on Steam with a complete trial arc and functional arena combat system. The modular behavior documentation became a reusable framework for the team, accelerating iteration on new enemy types in the later development stages.
